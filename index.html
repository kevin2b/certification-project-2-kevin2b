How to Play?

Click "Play" to start to play.

Each game is composed of 3 rounds. Click the buttons on your side to select "Rock", "Paper" or "Scissor".

Rock beats Scissor, Paper beats Rock and Scissor beats Paper. A win grants +1 point while a tie grants +0.5 point towards the game.

After 3 rounds, whoever has the most point wins that game, granting +1 for a win and +0.5 for a tie.

At the end of the game, you may choose to "Play Again" to continue or "Reset" to start over again.
