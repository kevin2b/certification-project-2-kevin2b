How to Play?

Click "Play" to start the game.

Each game consists of 3 rounds. In each round, select Rock, Paper, or Scissors by clicking the buttons on your side. Rock beats Scissors, Paper beats Rock, and Scissors beats Paper.

Winning a round gives you 1 point, while a tie gives both players 0.5 points.

After 3 rounds, the player with the highest score wins the game. A game win adds 1 to your win count, and a tie adds 0.5.

At the end of the game, you can choose "Play Again" to continue or "Reset" to start over everything.
